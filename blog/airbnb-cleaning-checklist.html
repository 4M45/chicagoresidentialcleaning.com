The Ultimate Airbnb Cleaning Checklist for 5-Star Reviews

April 28, 2026 By Marcus Rossi 6 Min Read

In the short-term vacation rental business, cleanliness is the single most important factor determining guest ratings. A study of review data shows that cleanliness is the primary driver of 1-star reviews. Even a single stray hair on a bedsheet or a dusty countertop can prompt a guest to leave a negative rating and request a partial refund.

To succeed as a host in Chicago's competitive vacation rental market, you need a systematic, checklist-based turnover strategy. Professional **chicago airbnb cleaning** teams follow a strict zone-by-zone checklist to ensure zero-defect results. Here is the ultimate checklist our lead specialists use to guarantee 5-star reviews on every turnover.

Why Professional Turnover Cleaning is Critical

Unlike standard residential cleaning, short-term rental turnovers must be executed within a strict 3-to-4-hour window between check-out and check-in. It requires a blend of deep sanitization, hospitality presentation (staging), safety audits, and inventory restocking. Below is the breakdown of the checklist by operational zone.

The 5-Star Turnover Cleaning Checklist

Zone A: The Entryway & Living Spaces

This is where your guests form their first impression. The space should look immaculate, smell fresh, and feel welcoming.

  • Dust all ceiling fans, lighting fixtures, and air vents.
  • Clean and sanitize high-touch electronics: TV screen, remote controls, and smart locks.
  • Pull cushions on couches and chairs; vacuum crumbs and debris underneath.
  • Wipe windows, sills, and glass sliding doors of fingerprints.
  • Vacuum and mop all hard flooring surfaces.

Zone B: Kitchen Deep Sanitize

Guests expect a kitchen to be completely sanitary. Any sign of previous usage will instantly lead to complaints.

  • Clear and sanitize all countertops, sinks, and backsplashes.
  • Clean appliance exteriors (refrigerator, stove, oven, toaster, microwave, dishwasher) to a streak-free shine.
  • Inspect interior refrigerator; discard left-behind groceries and wipe spills.
  • Wash and dry all dishes, mugs, cutlery, and cookware; stack them neatly.
  • Empty, clean, and replace trash and recycling receptacles.

Zone C: Bedroom Turnover & Styling

Comfort and hygiene are paramount in the bedroom. Sheets must be crisp, clean, and styled professionally.

"Never reuse sheets or pillowcases without a full commercial hot wash. Strip all bed linens down to the mattress protector and inspect for stains or hairs. Use a lint roller to ensure linens are completely lint-free."
  • Strip bed, wash linens, and replace with fresh sheets and pillowcases.
  • Check mattress protectors and pillow protectors; replace if stained.
  • Style the bed to hospitality standards: tuck sheets, fluff pillows, and place decor throws neatly.
  • Dust nightstands, dresser interiors, and closet shelves.
  • Inspect under the bed for guest belongings or trash.

Zone D: Bathroom Immaculate Detailing

The bathroom is the most scrutinized room in an Airbnb. It must be sterile, spotless, and completely hair-free.

  • Scrub toilet, including bowl, seat, hinge base, and exterior pedestal.
  • Clean shower walls, tub, glass shower doors, and grout to remove limescale and mildew.
  • Polish mirror, sink, and metal fixtures until spot-free.
  • Place clean bath towels, hand towels, and washcloths styled neatly.
  • Empty trash bins and sanitize them.

Restocking & Safety Audits

Cleanliness is only half the battle. You must also ensure that basic amenities are restocked for the next guest's stay. Replenish toilet paper, paper towels, dish soap, hand soaps, garbage bags, and toiletries (shampoo, conditioner, body wash). Finally, conduct a quick safety check: test the fire alarms and inspect the fire extinguisher pressure indicators.
